[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can hear the quiet things you never say out loud
The way you hold my gaze and pull me from the crowd
A single glance across the room is pulling me so deep
Your eyes talk softly, making promises to keep

[Verse 1]
The candle flickers, painting shadows on your face
I lean in closer, getting lost inside this space
You trace a circle on the table with your hand
We don't need words for us to finally understand

[Pre-Chorus]
The hour's getting late, the coffee's growing cold
But there is a fire here, a story to unfold
Every little breath you take is bringing me alive
I'm falling for the secrets that you cannot hide

[Chorus]
Oh, your eyes talk softly, saying everything we need
A language made of lightning, planting every seed
I'm hanging on the silence, swimming in your view
There is nothing I want more than disappearing into you

[Post-Chorus]
(Disappearing into you)
[Whispered] (Just me and you)

[Verse 2]
The midnight rain is humming rhythm on the glass
I want to make this single perfect minute last
You bite your lip and look away but then return
A slow and steady ember starting up to burn

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The hour's getting late, the shadows intertwine
I feel the heavy gravity of your heart pulling mine
Every little breath you take is bringing me alive
I'm falling for the secrets that you cannot hide

[Chorus]
Oh, your eyes talk softly, saying everything we need

[Post-Chorus 2]
(Disappearing into you)
[Whispered] (Just me and you)

[Bridge]
Oh, your eyes talk softly, breaking down my walls
A silent invitation echoes through the halls
I trace the gentle curve of your familiar smile
Let's leave the world behind and stay here for a while
This is Saeed, laying all my feelings on the line
I just want your quiet gaze to tangle up with mine

[Spoken]
(There is a whole universe hidden in your stare)
(And I'm ready to fall completely into it)

[Final Chorus]
